# CS50 Introduction to Artificial Intelligence with Python

This repository contains my completed projects from Harvard's CS50 AI course. Having previously worked with embeddings, vector operations, and RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) in production software, I took this course to formalize my understanding of AI fundamentals and fill in the theoretical gaps in my knowledge.

CS50 AI explores the concepts and algorithms at the foundation of modern artificial intelligence, diving into the ideas that give rise to technologies like game-playing engines, handwriting recognition, and machine translation. Through hands-on projects, I implemented:
